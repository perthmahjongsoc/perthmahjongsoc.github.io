About the Perth Mahjong Society

Credit to Felicia for progressing things from a group chat into an actual gathering.

Established 2023-06-25

Whereas certain people of Perth thought it good to assemble players of all abilities in a fine venue to play the game of Mahjong:

And whereas unspecified number of those present thought it good to form a Society:

Be the Perth Mahjong Society therefore established, this Twenty-Fifth Day of June, in the Year Two Thousand Twenty and Three.
